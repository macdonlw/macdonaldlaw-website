About Mark P. MacDonald, B.A., J.D. (LL.B.)

Mark P. MacDonald is the principal of MacDonald Law Office, serving clients throughout Eastern Ontario from the firm's office in downtown Smiths Falls, Ontario.

Called to the Ontario Bar in 2002, Mark MacDonald has built a practice focused on criminal defence, real estate, and wills and estates law. With over two decades of local experience, he offers practical courtroom advocacy, clear navigation of complex legal processes, and straightforward communication. Mark ensures every client understands their options and receives strong advocacy at every stage.

The Firm

MacDonald Law Office is a client-focused practice committed to providing responsive, high-quality legal services with transparent pricing and flexible scheduling. The firm serves clients in Smiths Falls, Perth, Brockville, and communities across Lanark County and Leeds & Grenville County.

The firm handles matters at the Ontario Court of Justice and Superior Court of Justice in Perth, Brockville, and surrounding jurisdictions.
