Trusted Criminal Defence, Real Estate & Estate Law

MacDonald Law Office, situated in downtown Smiths Falls, Ontario, delivers focused legal services in criminal defence, real estate transactions, and estate planning for clients across Eastern Ontario. Since 2002, Mark MacDonald has provided over two decades of experience, offering practical courtroom advocacy, clear navigation of complex processes such as Criminal Code and Provincial Act charges, residential conveyancing for purchases, sales, and mortgages, and will preparation.

The firm emphasizes a client-centered approach with straightforward communication, transparent pricing, and flexible scheduling. Serving Lanark, Leeds & Grenville, and nearby communities, MacDonald Law Office helps individuals tackle demanding legal matters through composed expertise and effective, results-driven strategies.
