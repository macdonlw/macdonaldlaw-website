About the Firm

Small Firm.
Big Commitment.

MacDonald Law Office is a small, downtown Smiths Falls practice. When you hire us, you work directly with your lawyer — not a revolving door of associates. That's the point.

Mark has practised in Smiths Falls since 2002 — first under the mentorship of the late Jack Kirkland, and then on his own as MacDonald Law Office. For criminal defence and wills, Mark handles your file personally. For real estate, he works with an experienced team of staff to move your transaction through smoothly.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What areas of law does MacDonald Law Office practise?
The firm focuses on three core practice areas: real estate law (purchases, sales, mortgages, and title matters), criminal defence (all areas of Criminal Code matters), and wills and powers of attorney.
What geographic area does the firm serve?
Based in Smiths Falls, Ontario, the firm serves clients throughout Lanark County, Leeds and Grenville County, and surrounding communities in Eastern Ontario. Mark appears regularly at courthouses in Perth, Brockville, and other regional locations.
Does the firm accept Legal Aid Ontario certificates?
MacDonald Law Office accepts selected Legal Aid Ontario certificates for criminal matters. Contact the office to discuss your situation.
