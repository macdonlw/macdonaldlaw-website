Criminal Defence

MacDonald Law Office provides experienced defence representation for individuals facing criminal charges across Eastern Ontario. Mark P. MacDonald appears regularly in the Ontario Court of Justice and Superior Court of Justice in Perth, Brockville, and beyond.

Areas of Defence Include:

  • Impaired driving / over 80 / refusal (DUI)
  • Assault and domestic assault
  • Theft, fraud, and property offences
  • Drug offences (possession, trafficking)
  • Weapons offences
  • Bail hearings (available 24/7)
  • Youth criminal justice matters
  • Regulatory and provincial offences
  • Appeals
  • Record suspensions (pardons)

What to Expect:

Every case begins with a thorough review of the evidence, including police reports, witness statements, and disclosure materials. Mark P. MacDonald will explain the strengths and weaknesses of your case honestly and develop a strategy aimed at the best possible outcome — whether that means negotiation, a trial, or a withdrawal of charges.

If you have been charged with a criminal offence, contact MacDonald Law Office immediately. Early legal advice protects your rights.

Real Estate Law

MacDonald Law Office handles residential and commercial real estate transactions throughout Eastern Ontario. The firm works closely with real estate agents, mortgage brokers, and lenders to ensure a smooth closing.

Services Include:

  • Purchase and sale of residential property
  • Purchase and sale of commercial property
  • Mortgage refinancing and transfers
  • Title searches and title insurance
  • Transfers of title between family members
  • Severances and land division applications
  • Easements and rights of way

What to Expect:

When you retain MacDonald Law Office for a real estate transaction, you will receive clear communication about timelines, costs, and any issues that arise during the title search. The firm aims to make the closing process straightforward and stress-free.

Wills & Estates

Planning for the future protects your family and ensures your wishes are honoured. MacDonald Law Office provides practical, plain-language estate planning services tailored to your circumstances.

Services Include:

  • Last wills and testaments
  • Powers of attorney for property
  • Powers of attorney for personal care
  • Estate planning and asset protection
  • Estate administration and probate
  • Estate trustee applications
  • Dependant support claims

What to Expect:

Mark P. MacDonald will meet with you to understand your family situation, assets, and goals, and then prepare documents that reflect your wishes clearly and in compliance with Ontario law. If you are dealing with the estate of a loved one, the firm can guide you through the probate and administration process step by step.
